Major Research Projects

Turbulent Resilient FSO Systems

Duration: 2 years Agency: Department of Telecommunications

Development of advanced Free Space Optical (FSO) communication systems with enhanced turbulence resilience for reliable long-distance optical communication links.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit

Light-Fidelity Networks

Duration: 3 years Agency: Department of Telecommunications

Research and development of Light-Fidelity (Li-Fi) network infrastructure for high-speed wireless communication using visible light technology.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit

Development of LiFi Networks

Duration: 2 years Agency: Department of Telecommunications

Comprehensive development of LiFi network protocols, hardware systems, and deployment strategies for next-generation indoor communication networks.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit

Development of VLC Network

Duration: 2 years Agency: NavTech

Industrial development of Visible Light Communication (VLC) network infrastructure for maritime and navigation applications.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit

RF Network Stack

Duration: 2 years Agency: Probus

Development of comprehensive RF network stack solutions for advanced radio frequency communication systems and protocols.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit

Next-generation Indoor Communication Networks (NICNet)

Duration: 3 years Agency: Department of Science and Technology

Development of advanced indoor communication networks integrating multiple technologies for seamless connectivity in indoor environments.

PI: Abhishek Dixit | Co-PI: Amol Choudhary

Converged Green Broadband Access (CONE)

Duration: 3 years Agency: Department of Science and Technology

Research on energy-efficient converged broadband access networks with focus on green technologies and sustainable communication infrastructure.

Principal Investigator: Abhishek Dixit
