About

Dr. Dixit conducts research in optical communication systems, visible light communication, and photonics. He has mentored researchers across academia and industry and published extensively in leading journals and conferences

Research Interests

  • Optical Communications & Networking
  • Visible Light Communication (VLC) & Li-Fi
  • Free Space Optical Communication
  • Radio-over-Fiber Networks
  • Next-Generation Indoor Networks
  • Passive Optical Networks
  • SIGNAL PROCESSING
