Research Areas

Leading research in optical communications, networking, and advanced communication technologies

Current Research Areas

Optical Communications & Networking

Leading research in optical communication systems, fiber-optic networks, and next-generation optical access technologies including passive optical networks and wavelength division multiplexing.

Visible Light Communication (VLC) & Li-Fi

Pioneering research in visible light communication systems, Li-Fi networks, and optical wireless communication for indoor positioning and high-speed data transmission.

Free Space Optical Communication

Advanced research in free space optical systems, atmospheric turbulence mitigation, and error correction techniques for reliable wireless optical communication links.

Radio-over-Fiber Networks

Developing advanced radio-over-fiber architectures for 5G fronthaul networks, wireless communication systems, and converged optical-wireless networks.

Next-Generation Indoor Networks

Research on advanced indoor communication networks integrating multiple wireless and optical technologies for seamless connectivity and high-speed data transmission.
